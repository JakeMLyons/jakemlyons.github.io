How to play

  • Make choices — click a numbered button to advance the story.
  • Save progress — click Save to store your game in this browser. Saves persist across sessions.
  • Load a save — click Load to resume from a previous save, or upload a .json save file.
  • Download save — use the Download button in the Load panel to back up a save as a file.
  • Inventory — items you carry appear in the HUD. Click an item name to see its description.
  • Journal — expand the Journal panel to read notes your character has gathered.
  • History — expand the History panel to see all scenes you have visited.
  • Restart — start over from the beginning (your saves are preserved).
Restart from the beginning? You will lose progress since your last save.
